A Short Biography

The Early Years

I was born on May 18, 2001 at 12:34 in the afternoon, at the now closed McKellar Hospital here in Thunder Bay.

I moved around alot over the course of my life, so far ive been to over 7 different schools so far.

Here is a list of public schools ive been to, in order from earliest to most recent.

  1. Mikinaak Onigaming School
  2. Five Mile Elementary School
  3. After Five Mile, i moved to the Toronto area and my next school was Robert Baldwin Elementary School
  4. W.I Dick Secondary School
  5. Bruce Trail School
  6. After this, i moved back to Thunder Bay and went to Hammarskjold High School
  7. Due to circumstances, i had to move to my mom's reserve and i went to Baibombeh anishnawbe school for grade 10 and 11
  8. In grade 12 i was back in Hammarskjold again

The Middle Years

I grew up at many different places during my life so far due to my family moving alot. ive lived from near a big city to the middle of the bush on a reserve.

So far in my life i haved moved to over 11 different houses over the course of my 19 years of life so far.

The earliest place i remember living in was at Sabaskong First Nation, but we didn't live there for long.

The nesx place after that we moved to Thunder Bay for a bit, but due to my family struggling to find employment we moved to Toronto shortly after.

I Toronto I lived in 3 different houses over the course of 5 years, and shortly after a while my Dad was getting tired from the 3-4 hour commutes so we moved back to thunder Bay.

After moving back to Toronto i went to Hammarskjold High School for a year, but due to circumstances i had to go to Baibombeh Anishnabe School for grade 10 and 11.

After living there for 2-3 years i moved back to Thunder Bay for the 3rd time and i finished grade 12 at Hammarskjold High School.

Where i am now

Currently i am living in Thunder Bay, Ontario and i am currently enrolled at Confederation College in the Computer Programminng course.

i plan on taking this course for the next two years as i enjoy it.

Future Prospects

After im done my Computer Programming course i think i would love to go into a Video Game Development course for a degree.

and after that i would like my career to be a Video Game Programmer.
